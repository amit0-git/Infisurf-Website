Infisurf is a Hostel Wifi Login tool - it is designed to access hostel wifi without logging in through browser. The Hostel/College Wifi logs out when the system is inactive for some time.
Tired of logging in again and again to access your hostel/college WiFi? We've got you covered! Say goodbye to those annoying and time-consuming login procedures and enjoy unlimited WiFi access without any hassle.

Usage
  1. Connect to College/Hostel wifi
  2. Open Infisurf and go to connect
  3. Enter Username and Password of Wifi
  4. Press Login and Enjoy :-)
System Requirements
Windows 8 or later. Once downloaded, the application is ready to use.
